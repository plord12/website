# PM 2.5 today

Particulate matter mass concentration 0.3µm to 2.5µm.

European Air Quality index defines the following levels for particles less than 2.5µm :

* 0-10 µg/m³ Very good
* 10-20 µg/m³ Good
* 20-25 µg/m³ Medium
* 25-50 µg/m³ Poor
* 50-75 µg/m³ Very poor
* 75-800 µg/m³ Extremely poor
